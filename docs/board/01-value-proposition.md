## How Jazebeh Earns Revenue

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    subgraph Sources["Revenue Sources"]
        R1[Brand tops up Wallet\nprepaid credit]
        R2[Campaign runs\ncost deducted per send]
        R3[Agency special plans\nbulk / discounted tiers]
        R4[Dynamic pricing\nhigher demand = higher rate]
    end

    subgraph Controls["Platform Controls"]
        C1[Back-office sets\nbase pricing per channel]
        C2[Discount coupons\nfor key agency partners]
        C3[Refund on failed sends\nautomatically credited]
    end

    R1 --> R2
    R2 --> Revenue[Platform Revenue]
    R3 --> Revenue
    R4 --> Revenue
    Controls --> R2 & R3 & R4
```

| Revenue Lever | Mechanism | Scales With |
|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Wallet top-ups | Brands pre-load credit | Number of active brands |
| Per-send billing | Cost deducted per message delivered | Campaign volume |
| Agency plans | Negotiated bulk pricing | Agency client growth |
| Dynamic pricing | Rate adjusts to demand | Platform traffic & peak loads |
| Refund policy | Failed sends credited back | Platform reliability |
