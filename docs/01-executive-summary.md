# Executive Summary

> Jazebeh Platform | Fiscal Year 1404–1405

## What is Jazebeh?

Jazebeh is a behavioral-intelligence marketing platform that transforms bulk SMS/messenger campaigns into precision audience targeting. It combines anonymous behavioral profiling with a self-serve campaign panel, enabling brands and agencies to reach the right audience with the right message at the right time.

---

## What Was Delivered in 1404

| Area | Deliverable |
|------|------------|
| AI / Data | Behavioral event schema, K-Means/DBSCAN clustering, 1,000+ behavioral tags, Model Cards, evaluation report |
| Platform | Customer panel (agency + brand roles), back-office panel, OTP auth, advanced reporting, wallet/billing, support |
| Automation | If/Else reminder automation, UTM link shortener and tracker |
| Documentation | Architecture docs, ops runbook, installation guide, user guide, 2 pilot case studies |
| Pilots | Controlled campaigns across 4 categories: financial, cosmetics, health, fashion |
